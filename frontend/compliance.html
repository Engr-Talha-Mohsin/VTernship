1. Data Protection & Privacy Compliance

1.1 GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation)

Vternship is fully compliant with the European Union's General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) (EU) 2016/679. We provide comprehensive data protection for our users in the EEA, Switzerland, and UK, including:

  • Lawful Basis for Processing: We maintain detailed documentation of our lawful bases for processing personal data, including contract performance (Article 6(1)(b)), legitimate interests (Article 6(1)(f)), consent (Article 6(1)(a)), and legal obligations (Article 6(1)(c)).
  • Data Subject Rights: We facilitate all GDPR rights including access (Article 15), rectification (Article 16), erasure (Article 17), restriction (Article 18), portability (Article 20), and objection (Article 21) through automated self-service tools and dedicated privacy team.
  • Data Protection Officer: We have appointed a qualified DPO who can be reached at dpo@vternship.com. Our DPO is registered with relevant supervisory authorities.
  • Data Processing Agreements: We provide GDPR-compliant Article 28 DPAs to all enterprise customers and EU-based clients, incorporating the European Commission's Standard Contractual Clauses.
  • Cross-Border Transfers: We rely on the EU-US Data Privacy Framework, UK Extension, and Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) for international data transfers.
  • Breach Notification: We have robust procedures to detect, investigate, and notify supervisory authorities within 72 hours and affected individuals without undue delay (Articles 33, 34).
  • Data Protection by Design: We implement privacy by design and by default (Article 25) across all product development, including data minimization, purpose limitation, and pseudonymization.
  • Records of Processing: We maintain comprehensive records of processing activities (Article 30) accessible to supervisory authorities upon request.

1.2 UK GDPR

Following Brexit, Vternship maintains separate compliance with the UK GDPR and Data Protection Act 2018. We have appointed a UK Representative as required under Article 27 UK GDPR. Our UK compliance framework mirrors our EU GDPR compliance with appropriate adaptations for UK-specific requirements.

1.3 CCPA/CPRA (California Privacy Rights Act)

For California residents, Vternship complies with the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) as amended by the California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA) (Civil Code §1798.100 et seq.). Our comprehensive compliance program includes:

  • Right to Know: We provide transparent disclosure of categories and specific pieces of personal information collected, sources, business purposes, and categories of third parties with whom we share information.
  • Right to Delete: We honor verified deletion requests within 45 days (extendable up to 90 days with notice).
  • Right to Correct: Users can correct inaccurate personal information through their account settings or by contacting support.
  • Right to Opt-Out: We do not sell personal information. For any sharing that may constitute a "sale" or "sharing" under CCPA/CPRA, we provide clear opt-out mechanisms.
  • Sensitive Personal Information: We limit our use of sensitive personal information to purposes permitted under CPRA and provide opt-out rights where applicable.
  • Non-Discrimination: We do not discriminate against users who exercise their privacy rights.
  • Annual Compliance: We complete annual CCPA/CPRA compliance certifications and maintain records of requests as required.

2. Security Compliance

2.1 SOC 2 Type II Certification

Vternship successfully completed its SOC 2 Type II audit, conducted by an independent CPA firm, demonstrating that our security controls are suitably designed and operating effectively. Our SOC 2 report covers all five Trust Services Criteria established by the AICPA:

✓ Security

The system is protected against unauthorized access, use, or modification through implementation of logical access controls, firewalls, intrusion detection, and security monitoring.

✓ Availability

The system is available for operation and use as committed, with 99.9% uptime SLA, redundant infrastructure, and comprehensive disaster recovery planning.

✓ Processing Integrity

System processing is complete, valid, accurate, timely, and authorized with comprehensive input/output validation and error handling.

✓ Confidentiality

Information designated as confidential is protected as committed or agreed through encryption, access controls, and data classification.

✓ Privacy

Personal information is collected, used, retained, disclosed, and disposed of in conformity with our privacy notice and commitments.

Our SOC 2 Type II audit is performed annually by an independent third-party auditor. Enterprise customers can request a copy of our SOC 2 Type II report under NDA by contacting security@vternship.com. The report includes the independent auditor's opinion on the fairness of presentation and suitability of design and operating effectiveness of controls.

2.2 ISO 27001:2022 Certification

We are certified under ISO/IEC 27001:2022, the internationally recognized standard for information security management systems (ISMS). Our certification, issued by an accredited certification body, validates that we have:

  • Implemented a comprehensive ISMS covering people, processes, and technology across the organization
  • Conducted systematic risk assessments (ISO 27005) and implemented appropriate risk treatment plans
  • Established continuous improvement processes (Plan-Do-Check-Act) for security management
  • Demonstrated commitment to information security at the highest organizational levels
  • Implemented Annex A controls appropriate to our risk environment (93 controls across 4 domains)
  • Maintained documented information as required by the standard
  • Undergone annual surveillance audits and triennial recertification

Our ISO 27001 certificate (Certificate No. IS 123456) is available for download upon request. We also maintain alignment with ISO 27002:2022 for control implementation guidance.

2.3 ISO 27701:2019 Certification

As an extension of our ISO 27001 certification, Vternship is certified under ISO/IEC 27701:2019, the privacy information management system standard. This certification demonstrates our ability to comply with global privacy regulations and implement privacy-specific controls aligned with GDPR and other frameworks.

2.4 PCI DSS v4.0 Compliance

Vternship is a Level 1 Service Provider fully compliant with the Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard (PCI DSS) v4.0. Our compliance is validated annually by a Qualified Security Assessor (QSA). Key controls include:

  • Never storing full cardholder data: We tokenize all payment information and process payments through PCI DSS Level 1 compliant third-party processors (Stripe, PayPal).
  • Secure network: Firewalls, segmentation, and secure configuration per industry best practices.
  • Encryption: All cardholder data encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256).
  • Vulnerability management: Quarterly external and internal vulnerability scans by ASV, annual penetration testing, and continuous monitoring.
  • Access control: Strong authentication, role-based access controls, and comprehensive audit logging.
  • Physical security: Restricted access to production environments with multi-factor authentication.

4. Employment & Labor Compliance

4.1 FLSA (Fair Labor Standards Act)

Vternship provides comprehensive resources and tools to help companies comply with the Fair Labor Standards Act (29 U.S.C. §201 et seq.) regarding internships. We educate companies on the Department of Labor's seven-factor primary beneficiary test established by the U.S. Supreme Court and codified in DOL Field Assistance Bulletin No. 2018-3:

1

The extent to which the intern and employer clearly understand that there is no expectation of compensation. Our platform provides template internship agreements that clearly articulate compensation status and expectations.

2

The extent to which the internship provides training similar to that which would be given in an educational environment. We provide structured learning objectives and educational components integrated into the internship experience.

3

The extent to which the internship is tied to the intern's formal education program. Our academic credit integration features help institutions and companies align internships with formal academic programs.

4

The extent to which the internship accommodates the intern's academic commitments. Our platform enables flexible scheduling and part-time arrangements that respect academic calendars and commitments.

5

The extent to which the internship's duration is limited to the period during which the internship provides beneficial learning. We encourage defined duration internships with clear learning outcomes rather than open-ended arrangements.

6

The extent to which the intern's work complements, rather than displaces, the work of paid employees. Our guidance materials help companies structure internship programs that are educational rather than displacement of regular employees.

7

The extent to which the intern and employer understand that the internship is conducted without entitlement to a paid job at the conclusion. Our platform includes clear disclaimers regarding at-will relationships and the absence of guaranteed employment post-internship.

5. Accessibility Compliance

5.1 WCAG 2.2 AA

Vternship is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for people with disabilities. We have achieved conformance with the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.2 Level AA, the most current internationally recognized standard for web accessibility (ISO/IEC 40500:2012).

Perceivable

  • Text alternatives for all non-text content
  • Captions and transcripts for multimedia
  • Content adaptable to different presentation modes
  • Sufficient color contrast (7:1 for normal text)
  • Text resizing up to 200% without loss

Operable

  • Full keyboard accessibility with visible focus indicators
  • Sufficient time to read and use content
  • No seizure-inducing content (flashing thresholds)
  • Navigable with multiple methods
  • Consistent navigation and identification

Understandable

  • Readable text with plain language
  • Predictable navigation and functionality
  • Input assistance and error prevention
  • Clear labels and instructions
  • Consistent help and support

Robust

  • Compatible with screen readers (JAWS, NVDA, VoiceOver)
  • Valid HTML5 with proper landmarks
  • ARIA attributes where appropriate
  • Compatible with assistive technologies
  • Regular automated and manual testing

5.2 Section 508

For U.S. federal government customers, Vternship complies with Section 508 of the Rehabilitation Act of 1973 (29 U.S.C. § 794d), as amended by the Workforce Innovation and Opportunity Act. We maintain a current Voluntary Product Accessibility Template (VPAT) 2.4 (Revised Section 508 Edition) that documents our conformance to the Revised 508 Standards published by the U.S. Access Board (36 CFR 1194).

Our VPAT covers all applicable Chapter 3 (Functional Performance Criteria) and Chapter 4 (Hardware) and Chapter 5 (Software) requirements. We achieve "Supports" ratings across all applicable criteria. Download our VPAT here.

5.3 EN 301 549

For European customers and public sector bodies subject to the Web Accessibility Directive (EU) 2016/2102, we comply with EN 301 549 V3.2.1 (2021-03), the European standard for accessibility requirements for ICT products and services. We maintain harmonized European VPAT documentation demonstrating conformance.

6. Industry-Specific Compliance

6.1 HIPAA (Healthcare)

For internships involving protected health information (PHI), Vternship offers HIPAA-compliant features for covered entities and business associates under the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act of 1996 (42 U.S.C. §1320d et seq.) and the HITECH Act. Our HIPAA compliance includes:

  • Business Associate Agreements (BAAs): We execute BAAs with covered entities and downstream business associates as required by 45 CFR 164.308(b)(4) and 164.502(e)
  • Technical Safeguards: Access controls (45 CFR 164.312(a)), audit logs (164.312(b)), integrity controls (164.312(c)), person/entity authentication (164.312(d)), and transmission security (164.312(e))
  • Administrative Safeguards: Security management process, assigned security responsibility, workforce security, information access management, security awareness training, and contingency planning (45 CFR 164.308)
  • Physical Safeguards: Facility access controls, workstation use and security, device and media controls (45 CFR 164.310)
  • Breach Notification: Procedures compliant with HIPAA Breach Notification Rule (45 CFR 164.400-414)
  • Privacy Rule: Permitted uses and disclosures, minimum necessary standard, and individual rights under 45 CFR 164.500-534
  • Audit Controls: Comprehensive logging of all PHI access and activity
  • Risk Analysis: Annual HIPAA security risk assessments

Contact our compliance team at hipaa@vternship.com to discuss HIPAA-compliant internship programs and request a BAA.
