How It Works

Your complete journey from signup to internship success

1

Student Signup

Create your free account on VTernship and build your professional profile. Tell us about your educational background, interests, and career aspirations. This helps us recommend the best internship tracks for you.

  • Fill out your profile with educational details
  • Select your areas of interest
  • Upload your resume (optional)
  • Get personalized track recommendations
2

Role Selection

Browse through our curated list of internship roles across Web Development, Data Science, Machine Learning, and UI/UX Design. Each role comes with detailed descriptions, requirements, and expected outcomes.

  • Explore available internship tracks
  • Review role requirements and expectations
  • Choose up to 2 tracks to pursue
  • Understand the commitment level
3

Skill Test

Complete comprehensive assessments designed to evaluate your current skill level. These tests cover both theoretical knowledge and practical problem-solving abilities relevant to your chosen track.

  • Take role-specific assessments
  • Complete practical coding/design challenges
  • Receive immediate feedback on performance
  • Get matched with appropriate difficulty tasks
4

Task Assignment

Receive real-world tasks based on your skill level and interests. Tasks are carefully curated by industry professionals and range from beginner-friendly to advanced challenges.

  • Get assigned relevant project tasks
  • Access detailed task requirements and resources
  • Set your own deadlines (with recommendations)
  • Ask questions and get guidance when needed
5

Task Evaluation

Submit your completed work for comprehensive review. Our evaluation system combines automated testing with expert human review to provide thorough feedback on your submission.

  • Submit your completed tasks
  • Receive detailed evaluation reports
  • Get constructive feedback for improvement
  • Resubmit if necessary to meet standards
6

Badge Earning

Collect achievement badges as you complete tasks and reach milestones. Badges serve as verifiable proof of your skills and accomplishments that you can showcase on your profile and resume.

  • Earn skill-specific badges
  • Unlock achievement milestones
  • Display badges on your profile
  • Share accomplishments with potential employers
7

Leaderboard Ranking

Track your progress against peers on our dynamic leaderboards. Rankings are based on task completion, quality of work, consistency, and community engagement.

  • Compete in track-specific leaderboards
  • Monitor your global and weekly rankings
  • Gain visibility among partner companies
  • Qualify for special opportunities and recognition
8

Internship Completion

Successfully complete your internship program and receive an official certificate. You'll have a portfolio of work, new skills, badges, and verified experience to jumpstart your career.

  • Receive completion certificate
  • Download portfolio of completed work
  • Get recommendation letters (if eligible)
  • Access job placement support and resources

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common queries

How long does the entire process take?

Most students complete their internship in 8-12 weeks, but the timeline is flexible based on your schedule and the track you choose.

Is there a fee to join VTernship?

VTernship is completely free for students. We believe quality internship opportunities should be accessible to everyone.

What if I fail the skill test?

You can retake tests after a waiting period. We also provide learning resources to help you improve before retaking.

Can I do multiple tracks simultaneously?

Yes, you can pursue up to 2 tracks at once. However, we recommend focusing on one track initially for better results.

Will I get a certificate?

Yes, upon successful completion you'll receive an official certificate along with all earned badges and your portfolio of work.

How are tasks evaluated?

Tasks are evaluated by experienced professionals using a combination of automated testing and manual review to ensure quality.
