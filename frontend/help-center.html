Frequently Asked Questions

How do I post an internship?
Go to Manage Internships and click "Post Internship".
How can I review applicants?
Navigate to Review Applicants from sidebar to view and manage candidates.
How do I update company information?
Open Company Profile and go to Account Settings to update details.
Why is my internship not receiving applications?
Ensure internship is open and skills requirements are correct.
